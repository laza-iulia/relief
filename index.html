Our Mission

Whether you are seeking stress relief, physical fitness, or a deeper connection with yourself, we are here to provide a space where you can find tailored guidance, a supportive community and inner peace.

Since 2003, our mission has been to make yoga accessible for anyone and to create a welcoming and inclusive space. It all began with a small, humble studio tucked away in the heart of a bustling city. Founded by a passionate yogi named Sarah, the studio started with just a handful of students eager to explore the practice of yoga.

In the early days, the studio started humbly, with a few dedicated instructors offering classes in a modest space. The community grew steadily as word spread about our studio, one where everyone, regardless of age, appearance, sexuality, fitness level or experience, could find solace and strength on their yoga mats.

In 2010, the studio expanded, moving to a larger space to accommodate the growing community. This new location allowed additional workshops and specialized courses. Sarah's dream of inclusivity became even more tangible as the studio welcomed individuals with diverse backgrounds and abilities.

In 2015, we took a bold step forward by embracing a new opportunity: that of taking part in yoga trips within the YogaEverywhere program. And since then, our studio has evolved so much that we have been able to even organize our own trips!

In 2018, as Sarah arrives to the studio doors one rainy morning, she notices a tiny cat that found refuge under our stairs. After days of gentle coaxing and leaving out food, the once wary cat became a cherished studio companion. Since then, Yogi Whiskerpaws' presence has become a symbol of the unexpected joys that unfold when openness and compassion guide your journey.

In 2020, faced with unprecedented challenges, our community stood strong. The global pandemic forced us to adapt and innovate. Live-streamed classes, virtual workshops and an enhanced online community became prioritary. Through these changes, we discovered new ways to connect, support one another, and foster a sense of unity in a time of physical distance.

Now, in 2023, our yoga studio stands as a testament to the power of resilience and community. The journey from a small, local studio to a globally connected community has been marked by unwavering dedication to our mission. As we look ahead, we remain committed to making yoga accessible to all, ensuring that our welcoming and inclusive space continues to be a source of inspiration, healing, and transformation for many years to come.

Meet Our Dedicated Staff

Sarah, CEO

"I wanted to pass on the joy and peace that yoga brought to me throughout the years."

As a CEO, Sarah encourages a collaborative and supportive environment within the center. She believes in the transformative power of yoga and is driven by the mission to make it accessible to everyone. Sarah's leadership style is characterized by empathy, open communication and a genuine concern for the personal growth of both her staff and clients.

Leah, Instructor for Beginners

"Anyone can master this practice and become more at peace with themselves"

Leah's classes are a blend of foundational poses, mindful breathing exercises, and a touch of humor to lighten the mood, taking the time to explain each posture properly. Leah's emphasis on proper alignment and the importance of listening to one's body fosters a safe and nurturing environment for those taking their first steps into yoga.

Here's what you need to know!

The process begins with a comprehensive orientation session where new trainers are introduced to the studio's philosophy, values, and teaching methodologies. Following this, trainers undergo a mentorship program where they shadow experienced instructors, gaining insights into class management, cueing techniques, and student engagement. Additionally, new trainers participate in workshops focused on enhancing their understanding of various yoga styles, anatomy, and mindfulness practices. To ensure ongoing development, regular feedback sessions and check-ins are conducted to address any concerns or questions. By fostering a supportive and collaborative environment, we aim to empower our new trainers to deliver high-quality yoga experiences that align with our studio's vision of holistic well-being.
